Making Your Own Instruction

The following three steps will guide you in creating your own new instructions. Though the hardware is fixed but you can manipulate the control signals to come up with new instructions. After creating new instruciotns you can execute them in the simulator and see how they work.

STEP # 1

You can choose from 2 types of instruction formats. You can either have an instruction with three register values or an instruction with 2 register values and one immediate value. This version supports only 1 instruction of each format simultaneously.
